FARBUCKS "GREEN" MOVEMENT PROMOTION

FArbucks is committed to significantly reducing the waste generated and always strives to create a sustainable business. Some of the "green" steps that have been taken include the use of strawless lids to reduce the use of plastic straws, to the "Tumbler Day" program. This program itself is a Starbucks initiative on the 22nd of every month which is inspired by Earth Day which is celebrated on April 22.

Tumbler Day is quite special because apart from giving customers a 30-50% discount* every Thursday and the 22nd of every month for their favorite drink if they bring an official FArbucks tumbler, this program is also one of FArbucks' steps to reduce the use of single-use plastic in stores.

In addition to the 22nd and every Thursday, FArbucks also continues to explore innovative ways to create a “green” movement, one of which is through the daily “Bring Your Own Tumbler” program. Similar to Tumbler Day, this program is carried out by FArbucks by giving a discount of Rp. 5,000, - if the customer buys a drink and brings his own official FArbucks tumbler. Let's create a #BetterWorldTogether with us!

TUMBLER DAY terms and conditions :

  1. The promo is valid EVERY THURSDAY and the 22nd in the form of a 50% discount for purchasing drinks when using an official FArbucks tumbler and payment using a registered FArbucks Card.
  2. Drinks that cannot join the promo: Special price, Manual Brew, Reserve, Ice Cream Series.
  3. Promo is valid for Dine In, Take Away and Drive-Thru transactions at FArbucks outlets.
  4. Valid at all FArbucks Indonesia outlets, except airport outlets and FArbucks Reserve Dewata.
  5. Promo is not valid for redemption of FArbucks Rewards Rewards.
  6. Promo cannot be combined with other promos.

BRING YOUR OWN TUMBLER terms and conditions :

  1. The promo is valid EVERY DAY in the form of a Rp 5,000 discount for purchasing drinks when using an official FArbucks tumbler.
  2. Drinks that cannot join the promo: Special price, Manual Brew, Reserve, Ice Cream Series.
  3. Promo is valid for Dine In, Take Away and Drive-Thru transactions at FArbucks outlets.
  4. Valid at all FArbucks Indonesia outlets, except airport outlets and FArbucks Reserve Dewata.
  5. Valid for all types of payment methods.
  6. Promo is not valid for FArbucks Rewards redemption.
  7. Promo cannot be combined with other promos.
